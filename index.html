SAMtoCIRCOS

Creates required inputs for CIRCOS visualization software for prokaryotic genome assembly from a SAM file. Additionally, shows GC content and nucleotide gaps in your genome from an optional FASTA file.

CIRCOS Visualization Software

CIRCOS Visualization Software (http://circos.ca/) is a software tool that allows for easy interpretation and visualization of data and information. Specifically, CIRCOS uses circular visualization, allowing for a very easy-to-read presentation of your data.

The Prokaryotic Genome

In single cell prokaryotes, hereditary information lies in the DNA or RNA nucleotides, thousands to millions of base pairs long. With such a large amount of information, it is often very difficult to process and interpret what is going on within your genome. With the SAMtoCIRCOS.py program, your information contained in a SAM file can be easily converted to the files required for CIRCOS visualization.
